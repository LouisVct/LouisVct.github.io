Welcome to Anatolia Kebab !

Established in 2005, Anatolia Kebab is located in the heart of Grenoble, at 49 Avenue Alsace-Lorraine. For nearly two decades, we have been delighted to offer you an authentic and flavorful culinary experience inspired by Turkish and Middle Eastern traditions.

At Anatolia Kebab, we offer a friendly and warm atmosphere. You can choose to dine in, take your order to go, or have it delivered to your home. We take pride in the quality of our ingredients, the generosity of our portions, and the excellence of our customer service. Whether you are a regular or visiting for the first time, we are committed to providing you with a memorable culinary experience. We look forward to welcoming you at Anatolia Kebab !
